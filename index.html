1 — Engagement

Clay Creative Agency ("Clay") will provide the disciplines listed above for the client named in this agreement. A detailed scope of work, deliverables schedule, and project plan will be issued within five working days of acceptance and will form an annex to this agreement.

2 — Process

Work proceeds through Clay's five movements — Listen, Discover, Shape, Build, Evolve. Each movement closes with a review milestone. Two rounds of refinement are included per milestone; further rounds are scoped transparently before any work begins.

3 — Investment & schedule

Fees are fixed against the agreed scope within the stated investment band. 40% is due on commencement, 40% at the midpoint milestone, and 20% on delivery. Invoices are payable within 14 days.

4 — Ownership

Upon final payment, all approved deliverables and their intellectual property transfer to the client in full. Clay retains the right to document the work for its portfolio unless confidentiality is requested in writing.

5 — Confidentiality

Both parties treat all shared materials, strategies, and commercial information as confidential, during and after the engagement.

6 — Withdrawal

Either party may end the engagement with 14 days' written notice. Work completed to date is invoiced pro rata; all materials paid for are released.
